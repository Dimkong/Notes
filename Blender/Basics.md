# Basics
## Controls
| Actions | Controls |
|-|-|
|Rotate| Press Mouse Wheel|
|Move| Shift + Press Mouse Wheel|
|Zoom|Mouse Wheel|
|Move Object| G|
|Rotate Object| R|
|Scale Object|S|
|Rotate on Object Axis| R + R|
|Move Object on Axis| G + (X or Y or Z)|
|Scale Object on Axis| S + (X or Y or Z)|
|Front View| Numpad 1|
|Top View| Numpad 7|
|Right View| Numpad 3|
|View Mode| Z|
